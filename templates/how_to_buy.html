How to Buy

Complete guide to purchasing and using virtual phone numbers for SMS verification

Step 1: Complete Authorization

Sign up for a new account or log in to your existing account to get started with our virtual phone number service.

Step 2: Top Up Your Balance

Add funds to your balance using any of the payment methods offered by our website. To access payment options:

  • Click on the profile icon in the top navigation
  • Select "Top up balance" from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your preferred payment method
  • Enter the amount you wish to add

Step 3: Select a Service

In the left panel, choose the service you need to receive SMS verification for:

  • Browse through the available services
  • If your service isn't listed, try using the "Other" option
  • You can reorganize the list by choosing country first

Step 4: Select a Country

Choose countries and operators with high OTP delivery rates for the best experience:

  • Check the "Statistics" section for delivery rates
  • Review the "Prices" section for cost comparison
  • Select the best option for your specific service needs

It is recommended to choose the countries and operators with high OTP delivery ratesâ€”check out the sections "Statistics" and "Prices" to find the best options for the service you need.

Step 5: Select an Operator and Click "Buy"

Choose your preferred operator and complete the purchase:

  • The phone numbers' pricing depends on the operator you chose
  • Click the "Buy" button to purchase the number
  • Your virtual number will be assigned immediately

Step 6: Apply the Phone Number

Use your purchased virtual number for verification:

  • Copy the Number: Press the copy button near the phone number, then paste it where required
  • Cancel Option: Click "Cancel" in case you do not wish to continue the verification process
  • Ban Option: Click "Ban" if the number has already been used

If you didn't receive an SMS, you will be refunded automatically. Rarely it may take up to 15 minutes.

Step 7: Receive SMS

Once you purchase a virtual number, you can receive unlimited SMS within the DaisySMS session duration. The number will remain active until you cancel it or DaisySMS expires the session. Some service/country/operator combos can provide you with only one SMS code.
