The basic use of this site is as follows:

  1. Add money to your account - Start by funding your account with your preferred payment method
  2. Choose a service - Select the service you want to verify from our services list (e.g. Microsoft)
  3. Get your number - You're given a phone number, copy and paste it to the desired site
  4. Receive your code - As soon as we see a message sent to it, the OTP code is displayed on the dashboard page
  5. Complete verification - Copy and paste the code to the form that asks for it on the site you're verifying
If you don't receive any message, money returns to your site balance. You can choose to get a short term (5-15 minutes) or a long-term rental (keep the number for as long as you want).
